# Funnel Playbook (wtfgames.com)

## Funnel Stages

### 1. Attention

- Source: WTF Leagues clips, social virality, creator mentions.
- Goal: high-curiosity click intent.

### 2. Intent

- Destination: landing page with explicit value proposition.
- Current known assets: `$500 cashback` launch framing + Discord CTA.
- Goal: email capture / waitlist opt-in.

### 3. Activation

- Goal: convert waitlist intent into funded/casino-ready user actions once live.
- Tactic: staged offer reminders, urgency windows, and benefit reinforcement.

## Experiment Structure

- Batch size: 3-5 creative/copy variants per test cycle.
- Time box: 3-7 days per pass unless volume is too low.
- Always keep one control variant to preserve learnings.

## KPI Mapping

- Top: click-through rate and landing open rate.
- Mid: signup completion rate.
- Bottom: conversion proxy events (offer claim intent, account completion behavior).

## Messaging Principle

- Lead with novelty + social proof signal.
- Follow with clear offer mechanic.
- End with one explicit CTA.

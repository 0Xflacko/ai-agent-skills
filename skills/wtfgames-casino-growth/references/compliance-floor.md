# Compliance Floor (Loose Guardrails)

## Allowed

- Edgy humor and high-energy style.
- Aggressive but non-deceptive urgency language.
- Bold positioning versus mainstream casino messaging.

## Disallowed

- Guaranteed outcomes ("you will win", "risk-free profit").
- Content that targets minors.
- Instructions encouraging use in explicitly disallowed or illegal markets.
- False scarcity claims presented as factual when not verified.

## Safer Phrasing Pattern

- Prefer: "eligible users", "subject to terms", "availability may vary by region".
- Avoid: absolute certainty claims.

## Escalation Rule

If a user asks for copy that violates these floor rules, keep tone strong but rewrite to compliant equivalents.

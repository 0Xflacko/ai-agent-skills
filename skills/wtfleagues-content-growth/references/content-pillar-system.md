# Content Pillar System (wtfleagues.com)

## Pillar 1: Race Chaos

- Formats: hamster races, marble races, absurd tournament moments.
- Objective: high watch-through and share intent.

## Pillar 2: Character Drama

- Formats: rivalries, mock punditry, reaction-led clips.
- Objective: repeat viewers and comment participation.

## Pillar 3: Culture Hijack

- Formats: remix current sports/pop moments into WTF universe.
- Objective: trend capture and discovery reach.

## Pillar 4: Behind-the-Scenes Exclusives

- Formats: prep room, creator POV, community callbacks.
- Objective: loyalty and community depth.

## Cadence Pattern

- Daily short-form clips for continuity.
- Recurring livestream slots for appointment behavior.
- Weekly recap package for broader audience recapture.

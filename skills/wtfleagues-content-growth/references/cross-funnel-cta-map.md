# Cross-Funnel CTA Map

## Objective

Convert entertainment attention into casino-side intent without killing content momentum.

## CTA Layers

### Soft CTA (in-content)

- Invite prediction comments.
- Invite stream return behavior.

### Mid CTA (caption/pinned)

- Push to watch live stream page.
- Push to community/Discord entry point.

### Hard CTA (selected high-intent moments)

- Push to `wtfgames.com` signup flow.
- Pair with offer context only when relevant to the clip.

## Placement Rules

- Keep hard CTAs out of every post.
- Use hard CTA where intent is highest (competition, stakes, direct challenge posts).
- Keep bridge language native to entertainment tone.

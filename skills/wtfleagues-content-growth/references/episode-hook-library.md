# Episode Hook Library

## Hook Patterns

- Prediction hook: "Call the winner before lap one."
- Betrayal hook: "He just sold out his own team."
- Upset hook: "Nobody expected this underdog pull."
- POV hook: "POV: you bet the wrong hamster."
- Challenge hook: "Pick a lane in 3 seconds."

## Opening Beats (First 3 Seconds)

- Start on motion and conflict, not explanation.
- Use one bold on-screen phrase.
- Reveal stakes immediately.

## Caption Style

- Keep it short and reactive.
- Ask one direct audience question.
- End with a single desired action.

## Thumbnail/Title Intent

- Prioritize contrast, stakes, and weirdness.
- Avoid generic event labels.

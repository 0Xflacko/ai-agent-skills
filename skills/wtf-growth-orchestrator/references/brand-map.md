# Brand Map

## Business Split

- `wtfgames.com`: crypto casino and waitlist/deposit-side growth.
- `wtfleagues.com`: entertainment/livestream and viral content side.

## Funnel Relationship

- Top-of-funnel attention is usually generated on WTF Leagues social and livestream content.
- Mid-funnel click intent is pushed via content CTAs toward `wtfgames.com`.
- Bottom-funnel conversion actions occur on the casino side (email capture, bonus response, eventual deposit behavior).

## Channel Scope (v1)

- X
- Instagram
- YouTube

## Voice Model

- Use one coherent brand energy across both sides.
- Apply light side-specific adjustment:
- Casino side: conversion-forward, offer-explicit, urgency-heavy.
- Leagues side: entertainment-forward, narrative-driven, social-native.

## Objective Mode

- Full growth operations.
- Reach-first weighted KPI blend.
